About me

Welcome to my profile!

I’m Biplob Das, a Senior Software Engineer, Intelligent Process Automation (IPA) Specialist, and Cloud Solutions Architect with over 12 years of international experience delivering automation, software engineering, and cloud solutions across Europe and Asia.

Holding a Master’s in Cloud-Based Software Engineering and certifications as a UiPath Advanced RPA Developer and Full-Stack Engineer, I have designed and deployed 200+ automation workflows, 20+ full-stack applications, and numerous enterprise cloud migrationsfor organizations like Sisua Digital, Neste, Elisa, and Tata Consultancy Services.

My solutions have helped businesses save 50,000+ man-hours annually and cut costs by up to 40% through automation, cloud adoption, and optimized DevOps pipelines.

Join me in my mission is to help organizations automate intelligently, migrate seamlessly to the cloud, and achieve measurable digital transformation. Whether you’re a fast-growing startup or an enterprise, I deliver scalable, efficient, and cost-effective technology solutions to keep your business competitive.
